OUR COMPANY
FLURO®-Gelenklager GmbH is a medium-sized industrial company based in Rosenfeld, in the Swabian region of 
Germany. Our family company founded in 1976 has established itself successfully on the international market as a 
manufacturer of spherical plain bearings and rod ends and now employs 170 people. We have been convincing our 
customers for more than 40 years with premium product quality, flexibility, speed and fair conditions.
